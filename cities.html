Cities of Golden Ring

Golden Ring is a ring of ancient cities situated a short distance from Moscow. Cities of Golden Ring are famous for their beautiful Churches and Citadels, as well as stunning landscapes with a countryside flavor.

Officially Golden Ring Russia includes eight cities:

  • Sergiev Posad
  • Pereslavl-Zalessky
  • Rostov Veliky
  • Yaroslavl
  • Kostroma
  • Ivanovo
  • Suzdal
  • Vladimir

But there are other cities near them that can be also worth visiting. If you look at the cities on the map, it becomes clear why the tourist route was called like this. Their location resembles a kind of a ring to the northeast of Moscow.

Best cities to visit near Moscow:

  1. Sergiev Posad
  2. Suzdal
  3. Vladimir
  4. Rostov Veliky
